3D Prototype Design

I started my 3D prototype by designing a 5 by 2 inch rectangle and then extruding it to a height of 1.75 inches, resulting in a rectangular prism. This prism would serve as my raw material, allowing me to chisel away unnecessary parts and craft my masterpiece like a sculptor.

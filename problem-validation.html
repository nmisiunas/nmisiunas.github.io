Market Research Findings
Source (APA Citation) Artifact Summary Artifact Critique
Smith, J., & Jones, M. (2022). Addressing Mobility Challenges in Rare Genetic Disorders. *Journal of Pediatric Therapy*, 15(3), 45-58. This study discusses the mobility issues faced by children with rare genetic disorders, including Coffin-Lowry Syndrome. It highlights the gaps in existing solutions and the need for specialized equipment. The article provides solid evidence of the need for better mobility solutions. It supports the validity of addressing mobility issues but suggests further research into user-specific requirements.
Davis, L. (2021). Market Analysis of Therapeutic Toys for Children with Disabilities. *Disability Studies Quarterly*, 41(2), 20-33. This paper analyzes the market for therapeutic toys and identifies the lack of options for children with severe mobility challenges. It provides data on consumer demand for specialized toys. It shows a clear market need but lacks detailed analysis on specific user needs for such products. It indicates the potential for product development but requires more specific research.
White, E., & Lee, R. (2023). Evaluation of Existing Solutions for Pediatric Mobility Aids. *Journal of Pediatric Engineering*, 12(1), 78-89. This review article evaluates existing pediatric mobility aids and highlights significant flaws in current designs, such as inadequate customization and functionality for specific needs. The article provides a critical review of existing solutions and their shortcomings, underscoring the need for more tailored solutions. It supports the project’s relevance but suggests exploring alternative designs.

3D-Scanning-and-Printing

For the 3D scanning project we decided to 3D model the steering wheel in order to make an adapter for the steering column that prevents the driver from continuing to drive the car.

Next, after we scanned the image, we put it into fursion and we created a prototype to be printed and fit onto the steering wheel.

In the end , the 3D scanning allowed us to have a perfectly accurate model that allowed us to make a perfect fit for our design.

First, I began by creating a half cube and creating two loops on the back of the cube to act as half of the hinge.

Next, I closed out the first body and created a new body to act as the top half of the hinge box.i reflected the same half cube shape up top, and then made the female end of the hinge with the pin running through it.

Lastly, I extruded the pin in the hinge to give it room to spoin within the hinge.

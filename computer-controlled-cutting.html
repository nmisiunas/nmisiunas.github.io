Computer Controlled Cutting

Wood cutting

1. First, I began by using the shapes tool on the right side of the screen, and creating a simple key shape, a square with a long rectangle coming out of it, and an oval in the square to acte as a hole.

2. Next, I used the knife tool on the right side of the screen and drew lines to carve out the shape of the key, creating a point and grooves.

3. Third, I selected the edges that I wanted to cut out, and deleted them, and I selected all the bodies, and combined them, and then after selected the key and the oval nad subtracted it so that the hole would be cut in the key.

4. Afterwards, I completed the same process with a circle rather than a square, and created a key with more grooves.

5. Following, I selected all my text again and chose the color tool on the right side of the screen and chose my desired color, white.

6. Sixth, I used the shape tool on the right side ofthe screen and made two ovals, one within the other. Then i used the knife tool to draw the lines for where Id like an opening in the ring.

7. Up next, I selected the edge for where I wanted the hole, and deleted it, leaving me with a key ring, and two unique keys.

Vinyl Cutting

1. First, I chose the shape tool on the left side of the screen and created a simple rectangle to act as the background of the sticker.

2. Next, I selected the rectangle that I would use as my backgroud, and I selected the color tool on the right side of the screen and chose the desired color, pink.

3. Third, I used the text tool on the left side of the screen and type out my desired phrase, then fitted the box to the size of the background rectangle.

4. Afterwards, I selected all my text, and at the top of the screen I chose to center the next, have it bolded and choose my desired font.

5. Following, I selected all my text again and chose the color tool on the right side of the screen and chose my desired color, white.

6.Finally, I selected the text once again and utilized the stroke tool on the right side ofthe screen and gave the text a border.

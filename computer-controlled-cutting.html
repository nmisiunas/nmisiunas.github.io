Computer Controlled Cutting

Wood cutting

1. First, I chose the shape tool on the left side of the screen and created a simple rectangle to act as the background of the sticker.

2. Next, I selected the rectangle that I would use as my backgroud, and I selected the color tool on the right side of the screen and chose the desired color, pink.

3. Third, I used the text tool on the left side of the screen and type out my desired phrase, then fitted the box to the size of the background rectangle.

4. Afterwards, I selected all my text, and at the top of the screen I chose to center the next, have it bolded and choose my desired font.

5. Following, I selected all my text again and chose the color tool on the right side of the screen and chose my desired color, white.

6. Sixth, I utilized the sketch tool to create a rectangle on the front of the vehicle measuring 0.25 by 0.12 inches. Then, I applied the pattern tool to generate a rectangular pattern across the front, consisting of seven rectangles evenly spaced 0.05 inches apart. Following that, I added a small circle, positioned 0.5 inches from the corner, to serve as the headlight. Finally, I selected all the sketches and extruded them by -0.2 inches.

7. Up next, I used the sketch tool to create an angled rectangle at the top of the main body to act as a windshield for the vehicle at about a 1 inch height.
